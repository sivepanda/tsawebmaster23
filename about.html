Space is the Next Frontier

Retrograde Space is the world’s first deep space tourism company. Created in 2021 by a group of aspiring engineers as an after-school club, Retrograde soon grew, as the founders moved through college, gaining degrees in aerospace engineering. After a few years of working in the industry, founders Hasan Venkatra and Cesar Iman decided to quit their jobs and live out their dream - to start a rocket company of their own. Their first project, Tian-LLV I, was a smashing success, successfully launching small satellite payloads into orbit. This success, though, was only part of Hasan’s and Cesar’s vision. Their end goal - to build the first ever deep space travel infrastructure, with powerful rockets designed to catapult human civilization faster, further, and deeper into space. Within a few short years, Retrograde Space successfully launched their first humans into Low Earth Orbit, then to the moon, as well as developing a space residency on the moon.
