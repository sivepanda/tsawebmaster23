Daedalus Lander

Daedalus is a strong and powerful capsule, capable of powerful and precise burns. The eight Hornet III-Mini engines, using our revolutionary fusion technology, pull a whopping 1,500N of thrust, capable of 360 degree adjustment on all axes in milliseconds.

Second Stage

This is the first stage outside of the forces of Earth's atmosphere. With 8,000 kN (in a vacuum) of thrust on the two Hermes HPE (Heavy Propulsion Engines) powered by our state-of-the-art nuclear fusion reactor cell. This stage completes the crucial burns to ensure Lunar Orbit insertion is precise and clean.

First Stage

This is the very first stage, the most power occurs within this burn. Using a combination of 4 Hermes MLEs (MicroHeavyLift Engines), 4 Hermes HLEs (HeavyLift Engines) and 1 Hermes SHLE (SuperHeavyLift Engine), each powered by the fusion of hydrogen atoms in the heart of the Hermes reactors, we achieve a jaw-dropping 90,000 kN at maximum thrust at sea level.
