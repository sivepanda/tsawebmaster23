First Stage

This is your transport module, where you will stay as you fly across space. Supplied with 3 powerful and efficient engines, this is the stage that powers most of your trip to the space station. The three Hornet IV-Superlite engines, using standard Methlyox fuel, pull a mind-boggling 600N of thrust, capable of 360 degree adjustment on any axis in seconds.

Second Stage

This is the first stage outside of the forces of Earth's atmosphere. With 4000 kN (in a vacuum) of thrust on the single Dragonfly CPE (Compact Propulsion Engine) powered by our state-of-the-art nuclear fusion reactor cell, this stage will be one that is tough to remember, as the crucial burns are completed in mere milliseconds.

First Stage

This is the very first stage, the most power occurs within this burn. Using a combination of 4 Dragonfly HLEs (HeavyLift Engine), each powered by the fusion of hydrogen atoms in the heart of the Dragonfly reactors, we achieve a jaw-dropping 40,000 kN at maximum thrust at sea level, setting its place as one of the world's most powerful first stages in a rocket, ever.
