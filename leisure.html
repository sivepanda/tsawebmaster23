Information

Retrograde is at the cutting edge of space travel. We aim to provide an unforgettable experience for all the right reasons. After booking your trip, our team of experts will take you through a quick screening to ensure we will have the amenities you need for a comfortable trip. The week before your launch window begins, you will spend a week in Cancun for a training period, where you will be taught everything you will need to know to have a safe and enjoyable trip. Staying at our 5 star hotel while using our state of the art training facilities will make this safety training course a vacation in itself. The trip itself will bring you a comfortable and awe-inspiring experience.
