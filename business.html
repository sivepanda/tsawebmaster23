Labs

At Retrograde, we support your continued growth by providing a rental program that can support your more out of this world experiments. Our rental laboratory program is ideal for customers who need laboratory space for projects requiring a Zero G environment.

All of our rental laboratories are high quality, state of the art, and cost-effective facilities.

Short-term rentals are available beginning with three-month terms, and long-term rentals begin with twelve-month terms.



These technologies include multipurpose facilities and Earth science facilities, as well as tools and systems for biology, human physiology, physical science, and materials research. Supporting research in both the external and internal environments of the station, our rental Labs also offer virtually uninterrupted worldwide data and communication capabilities.

Examples of available basic hardware and facilities include centrifuges, incubators, freezers, and microscopes. More advanced capabilities available upon request include rodent research (including bone densitometry), protein crystal growth, materials testing in the external space environment, and even DNA sequencing. For more details on currently available hardware and facilities, contact us here.

Welcome!
Welcome to our labs! Our state of the art facilities can be organized and retrofitted to accommodate any of your laboratory needs.
Storage
Our storage options include custom shelving that can be fitted with custom lighting with the function to support life systems, such as plant and animal growth.
Facilities
While simple at face value, our facilities can be expanded to include any equipment you need. If there's anything we don't have on hand, we will support its shipment to our labs!
Safety
Our lab facilities include facilities that allow for the safest possible ways for you to complete your experiments, from a forced air system that reduces the chance for standing air and toxic fumes to a "safety shower" that uses state of the art pressured water systems to ensure any contaminants are washed off quickly and safely.
Equipment
The equipment we have on hand is state of the art, from the smallest of microscopes, to our oscilloscopes and many more instruments are the best of the best, and always prepared to fit your needs!



Research onboard our rental Labs offers an unparalleled opportunity to understand how gravity influences physical and life sciences—exploiting these effects to understand basic phenomena and advance commercial pursuits. Our Labs work with interested researchers to identify the proper hardware, analytical equipment, transportation, and crew time requirements to ensure success of new projects.

All researchers are given complementary general safety training, as well as access to specialists in order to aid in ensuring safe lab practices and full knowledge of our built-in safety mechanisms and equipment.

Disclaimer: Due to the nature of an enclosed system on board our station experiments must be approved by our science team before they can be conducted in our labs.
